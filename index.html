Features
Everything you need to stay consistent

Customize daily goals, track streaks, and reduce friction — so planning your life doesn’t become a task itself.

1Daily goals

Pick the goals you want to follow each day: water, study hours, steps, workouts, sleep, or healthy meals with photo uploads.

2Streaks

Keep momentum with streaks per goal. Missed days break streaks — planned breaks don’t.

3Shared streaks

Commit with friends to the same goal and stay accountable together through shared progress.

4Vacation mode

Plan breaks in advance (travel, exams, holidays). Goals pause without punishing your streaks.

5Smart reminders

Set a custom time for each goal. Get gentle nudges exactly when you need them.

6Low friction

One place for correlated goals — no more switching between multiple apps to stay on track.

Why Daydoo
Designed for people who want structure — not rigidity

Daydoo is built for students and young professionals who are health-oriented, but overwhelmed by planning and inconsistent tools. Choose what matters today, stay consistent over time, and use friends for accountability.
